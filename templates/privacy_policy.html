1. Overview

MySalonCast API ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our AI-powered podcast generation API.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Content You Provide

  • PDF Documents: Text content extracted from uploaded PDFs for podcast generation
  • URL Content: Content fetched from provided web URLs
  • Generation Parameters: Podcast preferences, persona selections, and custom prompts
  • Request Metadata: Timestamps, task IDs, and generation settings

2.2 Technical Information

  • API Usage: Endpoint access, request frequency, and response times
  • Error Logs: Technical logs for debugging and service improvement

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Podcast Generation: Process your content to create AI-generated podcasts
  • Service Operation: Maintain, improve, and troubleshoot our API services
  • Content Analysis: Analyze source materials to generate engaging podcast content
  • Audio Synthesis: Convert generated dialogue into speech audio

4. Data Retention

  • Generated Content: Podcast audio and transcripts are stored for 30 days
  • Source Materials: Original content is processed in memory and not permanently stored
  • Task History: Generation logs and status are retained for 7 days
  • Error Logs: Technical logs are retained for troubleshooting purposes

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your content with third parties except:

  • AI Services: Content is processed through AI language models for generation
  • Cloud Infrastructure: Data is processed on secure cloud infrastructure
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or legal process

6. Data Security

  • Encryption: All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Access Control: Limited access to authorized systems only
  • Monitoring: Continuous monitoring for security threats
  • Regular Updates: Security measures are regularly reviewed and updated

7. Your Rights

  • Data Access: Request information about data we have about you
  • Data Deletion: Request deletion of your generated content
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your generated podcasts
  • Opt-Out: Stop using our services at any time

8. Third-Party Services

Our API may interact with:

  • AI Language Models: For content analysis and dialogue generation
  • Text-to-Speech Services: For audio synthesis
  • Cloud Storage: For temporary file storage

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. International Users

By using our API, you consent to the transfer and processing of your data in the United States and other countries where we operate.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date above.

12. No Authentication Data

Important: Our API currently operates without user authentication. We do not collect or store personal account information, passwords, or user profiles.
